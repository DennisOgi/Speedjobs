## ü§ñ AI Career Features

### 1. AI Career Counselor
**What it does:** A guided conversation that helps users discover their ideal career path.

**How it works:**
- User starts a session and answers questions about their background, interests, and goals
- AI asks follow-up questions to understand them better
- At the end, generates a personalized Career DNA Report with:
  - Personality type and work style
  - 5 recommended career paths with match percentages
  - Skills they need to develop
  - 90-day action plan to get started

**Where to find it:** Dashboard ‚Üí "AI Career Counselor" card or visit `/ai-counselor`

**Test it:** Start a session, answer the questions honestly, and review the final report.

---

### 2. Career Assessments
**What it does:** Professional assessments that analyze personality, skills, interests, and aptitudes.

**How it works:**
- Choose from 4 assessment types:
  - **Personality Assessment** - Discover your work personality (like Myers-Briggs)
  - **Skills Assessment** - Identify your strongest abilities
  - **Interest Inventory** - Find what careers match your interests
  - **Aptitude Test** - Measure your natural talents
- Answer 10 questions per assessment
- Get instant AI analysis with career recommendations
- Download results as PDF

**Where to find it:** Dashboard ‚Üí "Career Assessments" card or visit `/assessments`

**Test it:** Take at least one assessment and download the PDF report.

---

### 3. Resume Analysis
**What it does:** AI reviews your resume like a hiring manager and gives detailed feedback.

**How it works:**
- Upload your resume (PDF or Word document)
- AI analyzes it in seconds and provides:
  - ATS Compatibility Score (how well it passes automated screening)
  - Section-by-section feedback (summary, experience, skills, education)
  - Missing keywords you should add
  - Specific action items to improve
- Can compare resume against a specific job description

**Where to find it:** Dashboard ‚Üí "Resume Analysis" card or visit `/resume-analysis`

**Test it:** Upload a sample resume and review the AI feedback.

---

### 4. Interview Coach
**What it does:** Practice interviews with AI and get instant feedback on your answers.

**How it works:**
- Enter the job role you're preparing for
- AI generates realistic interview questions
- Record or type your answers
- Get scored feedback on each answer:
  - Score out of 100
  - What you did well
  - What to improve
  - Whether you used the STAR method (Situation-Task-Action-Result)
- Final readiness report at the end

**Where to find it:** Dashboard ‚Üí "Interview Coach" card or visit `/interview-coach`

**Test it:** Practice for any role and complete at least 3 questions.

---

### 5. Career Planning
**What it does:** Creates a personalized roadmap to reach your dream job.

**How it works:**
- Tell AI your target role and current skills
- AI generates a detailed career pathway with:
  - Step-by-step milestones
  - Timeline (weeks/months for each step)
  - Skills you'll gain at each stage
  - Recommended courses, books, and certifications
- Track your progress as you complete milestones

**Where to find it:** Dashboard ‚Üí "Career Planning" card or visit `/career-planning`

**Test it:** Create a pathway for any career goal.
