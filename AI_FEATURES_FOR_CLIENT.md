**What it does:**

**How it works:**

5. View your personalized report

**What you'll see:**
- Career DNA analysis
- Your top strengths
- Recommended job roles that match your profile

**Note:** This feature is available for premium users only.

**What you'll get:**
- Resume score and rating
- Section-by-section feedback
- Keyword recommendations
- Formatting suggestions
- Content improvement tips

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Complete Your Profile First**
   - Add your education, skills, and experience
   - The more information you provide, the better the AI recommendations

2. **Be Specific**
   - When asking questions, provide context
   - Example: Instead of "How do I get a job?", ask "How do I get a marketing job with 2 years of experience?"

3. **Use Real Information**
   - Upload your actual resume for analysis
   - Use your real career goals for planning
   - Be honest about your current skills

4. **Save Your Results**
   - Career pathways are automatically saved
   - You can download resume analysis reports
   - Take notes from the career counselor chat

5. **Come Back Regularly**
   - Update your career pathways as you progress
   - Re-analyze your resume after making changes
   - Practice interviews before actual interviews

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my information private?**
A: Yes, all your data is secure and private. The AI only uses your information to provide personalized recommendations.

**Q: How long does AI analysis take?**
A: Most features take 15-30 seconds. Career Planning may take up to 20 seconds for complex pathways.

**Q: Can I use these features multiple times?**
A: Yes! Use them as often as you need. Create multiple career pathways, analyze different resumes, practice interviews anytime.

**Q: Do I need to pay for these features?**
A: Most AI features are available to all users. The Career Intelligence Report requires a premium account.

**Q: What if the AI gives wrong advice?**
A: The AI provides general guidance based on best practices. Always use your judgment and consider consulting with human career counselors for major decisions.

**Q: Can I share my results?**
A: Yes, you can download reports and share them. Career pathways can be viewed by clicking the share link.
